Mission

Our mission is to improve and enhance the strength of the network in order to make it more resilient to both accidental failures and directed attacks. The network can only grow—and thus appreciate in value—if new users are joining it, and those users will only come if they trust the system. Ultimately, we believe that the BitSharesX platform can turn not only into a robust system, but also into an antifragile one. This can only be achieved if the system is able to withstand software anomalies easily enough to let the economic experiments be run in a smooth manner.

- DELEGATES -

To fulfill this mission, we propose 2 delegate bids, which can be seen on the home page. Collected fees will be used to:

  • first, pay for the hosting of the delegates and backup nodes on a good quality VPS. No cheap thing, this needs to run well and quality has a price.
  • then, the remaining funds will be used to fulfill each delegate's designated mission

- A HEALTHY ECOSYSTEM -

Ultimately, the question that every shareholder should consider is the following:
“Would you rather gain a small interest per year on your BTS, or would you rather see an army of independent delegates all devoting their energy on making sure that the network becomes even better than it already is, and potentially make the overall system grow more than the interest you would have had?”

The fact that the delegate pay can be quite high coupled with the fact that delegates can be voted in and out very easily and instantly from the wallet interface means the selection of delegates remains always competitive, and thus ensures good health of the ecosystem surrounding the DAC.
